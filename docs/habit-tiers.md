# Vitalist Habit Tiers

## Model

Each habit has 3 tiers representing progressive escalation of the same behavior or intent.
Tiers are about deepening commitment — not switching habits.

**Slot unlock logic:**
- Slot 1 always available (T1 habit)
- Slot 2 unlocks when Slot 1 habit reaches T2
- Slot 3 unlocks when Slot 2 habit reaches T2
- New slots always receive a T1 habit — you can't skip ahead
- Tier progression is auto-offered after 2-week trial, but always user's choice

---

## Natural Progression Habits

| Habit | T1 | T2 | T3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Walk** | 10-min walk | 20-min walk | 10,000 steps/day |
| **Post-dinner walk** | 10-min walk after dinner | 20-min walk after dinner | Walk + bodyweight resistance (squats, wall sits) after dinner |
| **Strength** | 10-min bodyweight | 20-min weights | 3× week strength program |
| **Fiber breakfast** | Fiber-rich breakfast | Fiber at 2 meals | 25g fiber daily |
| **Protein breakfast** | Protein-rich breakfast | Protein at 2 meals | 30g protein per meal |
| **Sodium** | Notice sodium labels | Stay under 2,000mg | DASH diet (under 1,500mg) |
| **Water** | 2 glasses of water | 6 glasses | 8 glasses / full daily goal |
| **Sleep** | Get 7+ hours | Consistent bedtime (±30 min) | Full sleep hygiene protocol |
| **Log a number** | Log one reading | Log daily | Review weekly trend |
| **Check blood sugar** | Fasting check | Check + log pattern | Share data with care team |

---

## Qualitative Escalation Habits
*Same intent and vibe, but a meaningfully harder version*

| Habit | T1 | T2 | T3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Deep breathing** | 5-min box breathing | 10-min guided meditation | Daily mindfulness practice |
| **Gratitude note** | Write one thing | 3 gratitudes + a reflection | Weekly life review journaling |
| **Morning stretch** | 5-min stretch | 15-min mobility routine | Yoga or Pilates session |

---

## Medication Habits
*Stay flat at T1 — adherence is the goal, escalation is a clinical decision*

- Take BP medication
- Take cholesterol medication
- Take diabetes medication
- Take HRT or supplements
- Log weekly GLP-1 dose
- Take all medications
- Check blood sugar
- Note any symptoms today
